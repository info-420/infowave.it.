01 What is Network?

It is set of computers by connecting two or more computers through by cable, They can share their DATA, INFORMATION and RESOURCES with each other.

02 What is the difference between IPv4 and IPv6?

An IP Address (Internet Protocol Address) is a unique numerical label assigned to each device connected to a computer network that uses the Internet Protocol for communication. It serves two main purposes: Identifying a device on the network. Locating the device to enable communication with other devices over a network like the Internet.

03 What is the purpose of IP address?

An IP (Internet Protocol) address is a unique numerical label assigned to every device on a network, serving as a digital identifier to facilitate communication and locate devices. It is used to route data packets to the correct destination on the internet, similar to a physical mailing address.

Services

Master the technologies that power the world. From fundamental networking to advanced networking, we've got you covered.

CCNA/CCNP

CCNA (Associate) builds fundamental networking skills in routing, switching, and security for entry-level roles, while CCNP (Professional) is an advanced certification.

Fortigate Firewall

FortiGate is Fortinet's leading Next-Generation Firewall (NGFW) platform, using custom security processors and AI-driven FortiGuard threat intelligence.

Palo Alto Firewall

Palo Alto firewalls are next-generation firewalls (NGFWs) that provide advanced, application-aware network security beyond traditional filtering.

SD-WAN

SD-WAN uses software to intelligently manage and route traffic across various connections (broadband, LTE, MPLS) for better performance and lower cost.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our courses, labs, and placements. Find answers to the most frequently asked questions here.

Does INFOWAVE IT provide practical training for CCNA and CCNP?

Yes, at our institute we focus more on hands-on lab sessions rather than just theory. You get to practice on real Cisco routers and switches so that you become industry-ready.

Which course are important to become a Network Engineer?

The industry gold standard for beginners is the Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA). and Cisco Certified Network Professional (CCNP)

Can students from a non-technical background learn networking?

Absolutely! Our courses are designed from basic to advanced level. If you have an interest in computers, we will guide you from zero to expert level.

What is the career growth and salary scope in networking?

Due to Digital India and increasing cloud adoption, the demand for Network Engineers is very high. You can start at an entry-level position and grow into roles like Network Architect or Cyber Security Specialist, where salary packages are quite attractive.

Does INFOWAVE IT provide lab access for Fortigate and Palo Alto Firewalls?

Yes. Security cannot be learned through slides. We provide access to virtualized and physical lab environments where you can practice Policy Creation, VPN Tunnelling, Threat Prevention, and SSL Inspection in real-time scenarios.

How does SD-WAN training benefit my career growth?

As companies move their applications to the Cloud (AWS/Azure), they are ditching traditional MPLS for SD-WAN. Professionals who understand SD-WAN architecture are seeing significantly higher salary packages and are often recruited for "Network Transformation" roles in MNCs.
